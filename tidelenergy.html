Tidel Energy

Tidal energy is a form of hydropower that converts the energy of tides into usable power, primarily electricity.

Tidal energy harnesses the kinetic energy of moving tides, which are caused by the gravitational forces of the moon and sun. It converts this kinetic energy into electricity using Tidal barrages (dam-like structures) or Tidal stream generators (underwater turbines).

Tidal energy development can support the "blue economy," which focuses on the sustainable use of ocean resources. This can include providing power for offshore aquaculture, research, and other maritime activities.

In essence, tidal energy technology is designed to capture and convert the kinetic and potential energy of moving ocean water caused by tidal forces.
